Introduction

Companies in India that import anything, need to maintain records of the good imported and disposed. There is a specific format that these records are maintained.

A monthly report is obligatory to be sent to customs, and once in awhile physical verification is done by customs officer on premises.

Since August 2016 Govt has made it mandatory for firms to maintain these records through a software solution. Software should be able to

  • Export the receipt & removal report for any month
  • Any modification to a record should be able to be audited

I would also like to disable the users who are not part of my team anymore, and reset their passwords.

Receipts Management

An accountant should be able to record receipts of imported goods, update receipt details to support correction or updating missing details. Deletion of a receipt should also be supported.

Any “Accountant” should be able to add/modify/delete receipts to the system.
