Must Read

This document evolves with the project. It covers all the details / conversations / agreements / videos / images - anything and everything that helps the reader to know the system.

  • Why the feature exists
  • What problem does it solves
  • What assumptions are made
  • What will be accomplished to accept the system as capable to solve the problems

There is no correct way to write this doc. As long as it can communicate these details and anyone who has access to this document can build the system, it serves its purpose.

A feature once defined does not ends there, as the knowledge improves, features will be refined, test cases will be added / removed and feature could be used to solve more problems. Also a change in context may lead to removal of a feature, as it no longer is useful.

Remember it should always be an snapshot of the image of the solution in its current shape.

A brief introduction of the problem and the solution

People all over the world end up wasting a lot of time in waiting. We have come up with this internet based solution, which will help reduce the pain to some extent.

Before we jump into the expectations from the solution, let's try to understand the people who are being served through this solution

Personas

Dolly is assistant to Doctor Naresh.

Doctor Naresh is an experienced physician who happens to examine many patients every day. It's not very uncommon to see long queues of patients who want to consult Dr. Naresh every day.

Lets meet a few more people who we will refer to understand the problems they face and what would they expect from any solution.

Aman & Shivani are a couple with a 3 year old daughter Ria and 7 year old daughter Smriti.

All of them are regular visitor to Dr. Naresh. Their consultation history is maintained at Dr. Naresh’s clinic. History is maintained manually on paper cards and indexed so that it can be retrieved within 5 minutes, provided the unique Id allocated to a patient is shared with Dolly.

Lets also know about  Rajat, who is the son of Dr. Naresh’s brother in law. Rajat is not comfortable waiting in Queue, and expect privilege due to family relations.

Ruchi lives at 30 minutes distance from Dr. Naresh's clinic, she would like to consult Dr. Naresh for the first time.

Expectations from Solution

I want to get an appointment for consultation and reach the clinic so that I don't have to wait for long.

 

I want to get the appointment between 11:00 am to 12:30 pm so that I can go after I am done with household work and come back before  my kids are back from School

I have to consult Dr. Naresh for Ria and Smriti often. I wish it's convenient to book the appointment without having to furnish too many details all the time.

I am a businessman, life is not very predictable, I want to go whenever I want. I don't want to wait for too long.

_____________________________________________

It's a pain to receive too many calls to book an appointment and answer calls to tell patients by when they should come. I wish I don't have to book the appointment and respond to status queries. I can than do something more productive.
